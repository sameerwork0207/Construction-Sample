About Nashik

Nashik is a city in Maharashtra, India. Located in the northwest of India, 180 km from Mumbai and 220 km from Pune. Known as the "Wine Capital of India", Nashik is witnessing rapid development and infrastructure growth, making it an ideal destination for residential and commercial projects.
